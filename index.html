About Saranya Boutique

Our Story

Saranya Boutique was born from a passion for preserving the timeless beauty of Indian fashion. Founded in 2010, we began as a small family shop in Chennai and have grown into a premier destination for sarees, bridal wear, and designer fashion.

Every piece in our collection is handpicked by our expert stylists, ensuring the finest quality in silk, cotton, and designer fabrics. From traditional Kanjivaram silk to modern fusion wear, we celebrate the diversity of Indian fashion.

Our dedicated team of 50+ professionals works tirelessly to bring you an unparalleled shopping experience — both in-store and online.
